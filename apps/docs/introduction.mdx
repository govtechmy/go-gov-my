Our mission is to reimagine the role of links – one of the most foundational pieces of the web – from being a simple **"resource locator"** (URL) to a full **attribution engine** – visualizing the user journey from the first click to the final conversion event.

## Features

Here are some of the features that GoGovMy offers:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Advanced Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    GoGovMy provides powerful analytics for your links, including geolocation,
    device, browser, and referrer information. [Learn
    more](https://GoGovMy/features/analytics)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Branded Links" icon="link">
    GoGovMy offers free custom domains on all plans for you to create branded
    links that stand out. [Learn more](https://GoGovMy/features/branded-links)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="QR Codes" icon="qrcode">
    QR codes and short links are like peas in a pod. GoGovMy offers free QR
    codes for every short link you create – both via our dashboard and
    [programmatically](/api-reference/endpoint/retrieve-a-qr-code). [Learn
    more](https://go.gov.my/features/qr-codes)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Personalization" icon="user">
    Customize your link's behavior with [device
    targeting](https://go.gov.my/help/article/device-targeting), [geo
    targeting](https://go.gov.my/help/article/geo-targeting), [link
    expiration](https://go.gov.my/help/article/link-expiration), [link
    cloaking](https://go.gov.my/help/article/link-cloaking), and more. [Learn
    more](https://go.gov.my/features/personalization)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team Collaboration" icon="users">
    Invite your teammates to collaborate on your links. [Learn
    more](https://go.gov.my/features/collaboration)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
